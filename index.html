about

Hi there, my name is Patrisha [pəˈtɹɪʃə]! I am a front-end web developer who takes pride in my ability to analyze complicated problems and generate creative solutions to solve them. I have a keen-eye for design while being mindful of accessibility and the user experience. It is my goal to work with a diverse, supportive and inclusive team who would give me the opportunity to foster my front-end skills as well as dive into some back-end languages. I have experience in a diverse range of industries including e-commerce retail, finance, fashion and restaurant, although, my educational background is in Intercultural Relations & French Linguistics.
